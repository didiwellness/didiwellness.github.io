About Didi Wellness

At Didi Wellness, we believe that true beauty radiates from a harmonious balance between inner well-being and outward appearance. Founded by Didi Heilmeier, a seasoned hairdresser with over two decades of experience, our studio is a haven where the worlds of hairdressing and Shiatsu seamlessly intertwine.

Didi Heilmeier's journey in the beauty industry began over 20 years ago, where she not only transformed hair but also connected with her clients on a deeper level. Through heartfelt conversations, Didi discovered that many of her clients were navigating mental and physical struggles. This revelation ignited her passion to provide more than just stunning hairstyles; she aspired to be a source of genuine support and healing.

Driven by this vision, Didi embarked on a path of continuous learning and personal growth. She undertook an in-depth course in Shiatsu, an ancient Japanese healing art rooted in the belief of energy flow and balance. Armed with this newfound knowledge, Didi's expertise expanded beyond the salon chair. She began integrating Shiatsu principles into her services, creating a unique blend of hairdressing and therapeutic touch.

Today, Didi Wellness stands as a testament to Didi Heilmeier's unwavering commitment to holistic well-being. Our studio offers a range of hairdressing services that not only enhance your appearance but also celebrate your individuality. Alongside, our Shiatsu sessions provide a profound sense of relaxation and rejuvenation, channeling the healing power of touch to restore balance in your body and mind.

What started as a heartfelt endeavor to support clients' mental and physical journeys has blossomed into a thriving sanctuary of wellness and beauty. As Didi Wellness expands, we continue to honor our founding principle: to foster beauty that emanates from within and resonates throughout. Join us on this transformative path, where beauty finds its roots in balance, and self-care becomes a cherished ritual.
